Aim

Our aim is to ignite the spark of adventure within every traveler and help them create unforgettable memories that will last a lifetime. We believe that travel is not just a means of escaping the mundane, but a way to enrich our lives, broaden our horizons, and foster a deeper understanding of the world and its people.

We are committed to responsible tourism and believe that travel should be sustainable and socially responsible. We promote ethical and eco-friendly travel practices, supporting local communities and preserving the natural beauty of our country.

Values

  1. Authenticity: We believe in promoting authentic cultural experiences and respecting the traditions and customs of the places we visit.
  2. Sustainability: We strive to be responsible travelers and promote sustainable tourism practices that protect the environment and support local communities.
  3. Customer-centricity: Our customers are at the center of everything we do. We are committed to providing excellent customer service and creating personalized experiences that meet their unique needs and preferences.
  4. Transparency: We believe in being transparent and honest in our communications, pricing, and business practices.

Cradle of Humanity

Lucy Ethiopia, a very old discovered skeleton is a surprising find not only for its scientific inputs but also for its complete body structure. Lucy is not only a skull of part of bone structure, but it is also 40 percent complete humanoid found in the world. Lucy Ethiopia is a 3.2 million old skeleton of evolving ape that was found in 1974 in Hadar the site of Awash valley, Afar, Ethiopia. Australopithecus Afarensis Lucy was the primary Australopithecus Afarensis to be found, yet there has been in excess of 300 disclosures of the species to date.
